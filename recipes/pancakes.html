Odin's Delicious Pancakes

Description

These are the pancakes that Odin has made in his own kitchen! These carefull crafted cakes are enough to fuel any god for a whole day!

Ingredients

  • One cup of bisquick
  • Half of a cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • A splash of vegetable oil
  • A sprinkle of vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1 tbs of melted butter
  • Blueberries (Optional)

Instructions

First, mix all ingredients together thouroughly, leaving no clumps or chunks. Then, preheat a stovetop or griddle to low-medium heat, and cover with non-stick spray. When heated, add the batter in fourth-cup increments to the griddle or pan, and let sit until the edges begin to bubble, about ~4 minutes. Flip over, and let sit for roughly the same amount of time, and cook each side until golden. Serve hot, with your choice of fruit toppings, butter, and/or syrup! Enjoy a meal fit for a god!
